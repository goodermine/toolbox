    FAQ

    Are my images uploaded?

    No. Everything is processed on your device with JavaScript (the open-source browser-image-compression library). Nothing is sent to a server.

    Which format should I pick?

    JPG — smallest, best for photos. WebP — even smaller at similar quality, supported by all modern browsers. PNG — lossless, supports transparency. Keep — same format as the input, just compressed/resized.

    Will it ruin quality?

    You control it with the quality slider. Higher = better looking but larger; 80% is a good balance for most photos. PNG is always lossless.

    Can I do lots at once?

    Yes — add as many as you like and download them all as a ZIP. No daily limit.
