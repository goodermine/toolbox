    FAQ

    Is my image uploaded?

    No. The AI model (U²-Net) runs on your own device via ONNX Runtime Web. After the one-time model download, it works fully offline — nothing is ever sent to a server.

    What works best?

    Photos with a clear, distinct subject — a person, product, pet, or object against a simpler background. Very busy or low-contrast scenes are harder.

    Why is the first run slow?

    The model and runtime download once (~18 MB total) and your device "warms up." After that it's cached and quick — and works without internet.

    What do I get?

    A PNG with the background removed (transparent). Drop in many at once and download them all as a ZIP.
