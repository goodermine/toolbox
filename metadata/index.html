FAQ

Why does this matter?

Photos often carry hidden data: the exact GPS coordinates where they were taken, your camera/phone model and serial, timestamps, and (for AI images) generation parameters or "Made with AI" tags. Anyone you share the file with can read it. This tool shows you exactly what's there.

Is anything uploaded?

No. Metadata is read locally with the open-source exifr library. Your images never leave your device.

How do I remove it?

Head to the home page and use the Clean Metadata tool — it strips all of this while keeping your image pixel-for-pixel identical.
