    FAQ

    What is HEIC, and why convert it?

    HEIC (HEIF) is the format iPhones and iPads use to save photos — it's efficient, but many apps, websites, and Windows PCs can't open it. Converting to JPG or PNG makes your photos work everywhere.

    Are my photos uploaded?

    No. Conversion happens entirely on your device using JavaScript (the open-source heic2any library). Nothing is sent to a server — you can even go offline after the page loads.

    Can I convert many photos at once?

    Yes — drop in as many as you like and download them all as a ZIP. There's no daily limit.

    JPG or PNG?

    JPG is smaller and ideal for photos (pick your quality above). PNG is lossless and larger — better if you need maximum fidelity or transparency.
